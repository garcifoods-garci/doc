# Raala Kithul E-commerce Website High-Level Feature Document (HLFD)

## 1. Project Overview

### 1.1 Business Objectives

Raala Kithul requires a modern e-commerce website to strengthen its digital presence, showcase its authentic Sri Lankan kithul-based product range, and enable direct-to-consumer online sales. The platform should support brand storytelling, product discovery, promotions, and scalable digital growth.

### 1.2 Target Audience

- Health-conscious consumers
- Young digital-first customers
- Families looking for natural sweeteners and traditional food products
- Sri Lankan local market customers
- Potential export and diaspora customers in future phases
- Retail, gifting, and wellness-focused buyers

### 1.3 Project Goals

- Build a strong branded online storefront for Raala Kithul
- Allow customers to browse, search, and purchase products easily
- Enable the business to run promotions and marketing campaigns efficiently
- Provide secure checkout and reliable order processing
- Create a scalable platform for future expansion, subscriptions, loyalty, and international commerce

### 1.4 Expected Business Outcomes

- Increased direct online sales
- Improved brand awareness and customer trust
- Better customer retention through account and marketing features
- Increased campaign conversion from social media and digital advertising
- Improved operational visibility across orders, stock, and promotions

## 18. Key Assumptions

- Initial launch will focus primarily on Sri Lankan customers and local fulfilment.
- Final payment gateway selection will depend on commercial and technical availability.
- Shipping policies, tax rules, and return policies will be finalized during detailed requirements.
- UI/UX design will reflect Raala Kithul’s brand identity, natural product positioning, and mobile-first customer behaviour.
- Promotions will be a central growth driver, especially for social media-led campaigns.

## 20. Conclusion

This High-Level Feature Document provides the foundation for planning the Raala Kithul e-commerce website as a scalable digital commerce platform. It is intended to guide stakeholder alignment, solution architecture, UI/UX design, development estimation, sprint planning, and future roadmap discussions.

The recommended direction is to deliver a strong Phase 1 e-commerce platform with core catalogue, checkout, promotion, and operational capabilities, while keeping the architecture flexible enough to support future brand growth, customer loyalty, and market expansion.
