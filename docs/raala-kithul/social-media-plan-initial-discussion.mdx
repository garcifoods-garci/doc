# Raala Kithul Social Media Plan - Initial Discussion

## Overview

This document captures the initial discussion for the **Raala Kithul social media plan**. The goal is to position Raala Kithul as a **2.0 social media brand** aimed at younger consumers, while traditional retail continues to serve the more mature 1.0 market.

## Brand Positioning

- **Social media is the new 2.0 market** for Raala Kithul.
- The brand should be presented as modern, healthy, active, and relevant to younger lifestyles.
- **Supermarkets are not the primary channel for this campaign.**
  - Supermarkets mainly serve the **mature and traditional generation**.
  - This traditional retail audience is considered the **1.0 market**.
- Social media should be used to build demand among the **young generation** and drive them directly to the website.

## Target Market

Primary target groups:

- Young generation
- Health-conscious consumers
- People in the fitness sector
- Gym users
- Sports communities
- Yoga communities

## Marketing Approach

### Influencer Strategy

- Promote through **social media celebrities and influencers**.
- Plan to work with **6 influencers over 6 months**.
- A **marketing company** can be hired to identify, manage, and coordinate influencers.
- The campaign budget is expected to come **from the EU**.

### Campaign Timeline

#### First 6 Months

- Launch the social media awareness campaign
- Partner with 6 influencers
- Build brand recognition among young consumers
- Push all influencer traffic to the Raala Kithul website

#### Next 6 Months

- Continue the campaign based on performance
- Strengthen conversion-focused content
- Expand on the most successful influencers, audiences, and content styles
- Increase traffic to the website using stronger offers and promo codes

## Website-First Strategy

Every video and influencer post should direct audiences to the **official website**.

Website goals:

- Convert social media interest into direct sales
- Offer promotions that encourage first-time purchases
- Capture younger customers outside supermarket channels

### Website Offers

Possible offers to feature on the site:

- **Sap 250ml** small portion packs
- **Flavoured variations**
- Website-exclusive promotions
- Promo codes linked to influencer campaigns

## Product Strategy for the Young Market

To appeal to the younger market, Raala Kithul should highlight:

- Smaller, more convenient serving sizes
- Easy-to-try product formats
- Flavoured versions for broader appeal
- A modern and active brand image

## Offline Promotion Linked to Social Media

In addition to online promotion, sampling and awareness activities can happen in lifestyle-focused locations such as:

- Fitness centers
- Juice bars in fitness centers
- Yoga centers

### Promotion Method

- Offer snack or product samples
- Encourage people to visit the website
- Use promo codes to connect physical sampling with online conversion

## Key Campaign Principle

**All promotional activity should lead people back to the website.**

This includes:

- Influencer videos
- Social posts
- Sampling campaigns
- Fitness and wellness partnerships

## Initial Direction Summary

Raala Kithul should be marketed in two distinct ways:

1. **Traditional / 1.0 market**
   - Mature generation
   - Traditional generation
   - Primarily supermarket-based

2. **Social media / 2.0 market**
   - Young generation
   - Health, fitness, and sport audiences
   - Influencer-led awareness
   - Website-driven offers and conversions

## Next Steps

- Confirm available campaign budget from the EU
- Identify and appoint a marketing company
- Shortlist 6 suitable influencers for the first 6 months
- Define website offers and promo code structure
- Prepare content that clearly directs users to the website
- Plan sampling in gyms, juice bars, and yoga centers
