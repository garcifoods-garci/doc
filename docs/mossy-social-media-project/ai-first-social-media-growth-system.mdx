# Mossy Fashions - AI-First Social Media Growth System

## 1. Project Intent

This document captures a smarter approach to building a **social media service for Mossy Fashions**.

The goal is **not** to operate like a traditional marketing agency that simply posts content.

The goal is to build a **repeatable, software-driven growth system** that delivers measurable business outcomes.

### Core Growth Formula

**More Attention → More Conversations → More Sales → More Repeat Customers**

## 2. Strategic Direction

Because of a software engineering background, the right model is to think like a **software company that sells marketing results**.

That means:

- build one repeatable framework
- use the same structure for every client
- measure the same KPIs for every client
- use AI for research, content, optimization, and automation
- gradually turn the service into an internal platform or Agency OS

Mossy Fashions should be treated as the **first structured client model**.

## 3. What the Real Goal Appears to Be

Based on the direction provided, the deeper goal is likely this:

1. Build a strong social media growth system for Mossy Fashions
2. Prove it with measurable business results
3. Standardize the workflow into a repeatable client framework
4. Turn the workflow into a reusable internal operating system
5. Eventually evolve that system into a productized service or SaaS platform

So this is bigger than one client page.
It is the foundation of an **AI-powered agency model**.

## 4. Delivery Model

The service should be built around the following layers:

| Layer | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Research Hub | Understand business, competitors, customers, and trends |
| Content Studio | Generate content ideas, scripts, captions, prompts, and plans |
| Publishing Engine | Schedule and distribute content across platforms |
| Engagement Hub | Handle comments, DMs, FAQs, and inquiries |
| Sales Funnel | Convert platform traffic into WhatsApp conversations and sales |
| Analytics Dashboard | Measure growth, conversion, and return on effort/ad spend |
| Optimization Loop | Use AI insights to improve the next cycle |

## 5. Phase 1 - Audit (Week 1)

Before content creation begins, audit the business fully.

### 5.1 Business Audit

Create a central client workspace in **Notion** or in a future internal web app.

#### Required sections

- Business goals
- Target audience
- Competitors
- Current products
- Profit margins
- Best sellers
- Seasonal products
- Brand personality
- Customer FAQs
- Customer objections
- Existing social channels

### 5.2 Social Media Audit

Create a consistent KPI structure for each platform.

#### Facebook Audit

Track:

- Followers
- Reach
- Engagement rate
- Best posts
- Worst posts
- Best posting times
- Video views
- Story views
- Click rate

#### Instagram Audit

Track:

- Reel performance
- Story performance
- Saves
- Shares
- Profile visits
- Website clicks

#### WhatsApp Audit

Track:

- Number of inquiries
- Average response time
- Conversion percentage
- Repeat customers

### 5.3 Audit Output

The output of Phase 1 should be:

- one structured client profile
- one social performance baseline
- one KPI dashboard
- one problem/opportunity summary

## 6. Phase 2 - AI Research

This is where the service becomes meaningfully different from a normal agency.

AI should be used **every month**, not occasionally.

### 6.1 Competitor Research

#### AI Research Prompt Direction

Ask AI to find the top 20 relevant competitors and analyse:

- Posting frequency
- Best-performing content
- Reels
- Hooks
- Hashtags
- Product pricing
- Customer complaints
- Customer reviews
- Content gaps

#### Output

Store all findings in a reusable research database.

### 6.2 Customer Research

Ask AI:

- Who buys these products?
- What problems do they have?
- Why do they not buy?
- What emotions trigger buying?
- What questions do customers ask?

### 6.3 Trend Research

Every Monday, run a trend check using AI.

Track:

- Trending audio
- Trending colours
- Fashion trends
- Sri Lankan trends
- Festival opportunities

### 6.4 Why This Matters

This prevents random posting and turns content planning into a data-supported process.

## 15. Build Your Agency OS

Instead of saying:

> “We manage Facebook pages.”

Build an internal **Agency Operating System** where every client follows the same workflow.

### Core Components

#### Research Hub

AI competitor, customer, and trend research.

#### Content Studio

Monthly content calendars, captions, images, and video prompts.

#### Publishing Engine

Schedule posts across Facebook and Instagram.

#### Engagement Hub

AI-assisted replies to comments and DMs, with human review for complex cases.

#### Sales Funnel

Click-to-WhatsApp ads, WhatsApp Business workflows, follow-ups, and repeat-customer campaigns.

#### Analytics Dashboard

Measure leads, engagement, sales, ROAS, and monthly growth.

#### Continuous Optimization

AI identifies what worked, suggests experiments, and improves the next month’s plan.
